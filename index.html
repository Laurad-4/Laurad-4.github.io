Political Campaigns & Advocacy

The digital era has changed the way campaigns get their message out to voters. Political digital marketing allows the candidate to reach thousands of voters. Campaigning is also more challenging during the pandemic since going door to door is not an option. We can reach your voters with digital strategies such as Geo and IP targeting. We have worked on multiple campaigns in different capacities at the local, state, and national levels.

Peer to Peer texting has gained popularity in recent years. P2P texting is one of the most effective ways to reach voters with personalized messages to GOTV, reminders about events, and encouragement about the candidate.

Email marketing is also a popular tool candidates can use to communicate with their base. Email marketing encourages engagement with voters so they can learn more about the candidate, sign up for events, for political polling purposes, yard sign placement, etc.

Nonprofits and Property Management

Covid has changed the fundraising landscape. Galas and events coordinated by nonprofit organizations have quickly pivoted to online activities. Digital marketing helps nonprofits keep in contact with your donors and keep your cause top-of-mind. We can create a strong social media presence, arrange online fundraisers, and keep your email marketing and blogs current.

Content marketing, web design, blogs, articles, and newsletters are all part of the services we can provide to assist property management and real estate agents reach their target audiences.
