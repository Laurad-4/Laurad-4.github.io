Social Media Management

We help define which social media platforms best suit your brand and establish a frequency of posts for your products or services. We manage your online presence on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, YouTube, Yelp, and others. Our strategies build brand presence, increase awareness and followers, and ultimately drive results.

We develop an editorial calendar for content sharing and keep your social media platforms current with scheduled status updates. We design cover images and build social profiles.

Sharing on social media at strategic times for the various platforms generates more engagement. We monitor your platforms and interact with users. Engagement is a key factor in a strong social media presence, and we take care of that for your business so you can focus on developing new products! We use hashtags on every post and listen and connect with your audience each and every time. Analytics results are also reviewed to ensure your brand is getting the best exposure.

We have attractive offerings tailored to your budget. Depending on your needs, we will recommend our gold, silver or bronze package. Contact us for pricing details.
