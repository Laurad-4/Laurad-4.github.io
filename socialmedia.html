Social Media Management

LD Digital Strategies helps define which social media platforms best suit your brand and establish the best frequency of posts for your products or services. Your online presence is seamlessly managed on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, YouTube, Yelp, and others. These strategies build brand presence, increase awareness/followers for your brand, and ultimately drives results.

We develop an editorial calendar for content sharing and keep your social media platforms current with scheduled status updates. We design cover images and build social profiles.

Sharing on social media at strategic times for the various platforms generates more engagement. We monitor your platforms and interact with users. Engagement is a key factor in a strong social media presence, and we take care of that for your business so you can focus on developing new products! We use hashtags on every post and listen and connect with your audience each and every time. Analytics are also reviewed to ensure your brand is getting the best exposure.

Tiered packages are tailored to your budget. Contact us for pricing details.
