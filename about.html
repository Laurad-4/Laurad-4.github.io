Laura began her professional career in the cruise industry while simultaneously earning her Associate's degree in business administration from Miami Dade College. She spent over 20 years with leading cruise companies including CCL, Cunard, Princess, and Seabourn specializing in National Accounts. Laura held the positions of Business Solutions Manager, Hotel Engagement Manager and Strategic Programs Manager during her tenure of close to 7 years at Travel Leaders Group. Laura later managed corporate travel hotel engagement at CCRA helping agents grow their hotel sales using the company's rate codes.

Laura launched LD Digital Strategies while completing her Bachelor of general studies at the University of Miami. Digital marketing is her area of concentration at UM, and she is using the skills she learned in her newly launched business.

In 2011, Laura was elected to the board of directors and served as president for the Courtyards of the Grove condominium association for four years. Laura has been active in animal causes, historic preservation/responsible development, political campaigns, LGBTQ supporter, and has volunteered with the senior community.

Based in Miami Beach, Laura is a Miami native who is fluent in Spanish. She lives on the Venetian Islands with her life partner, Mark Samuelian.
